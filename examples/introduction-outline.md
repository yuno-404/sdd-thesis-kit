# Introduction Outline Template

Use this sequence to draft your Introduction quickly.

## Paragraph 1: Broad Context

Explain the big problem domain and why it matters in real applications.

## Paragraph 2: Current Gap

Summarize what existing methods do, then point out their shared limitation(s).

## Paragraph 3: Your Core Idea

State your approach at a high level and why it addresses the identified gap.

## Paragraph 4: Contributions (3+ bullets)

- We propose ...
- We design/derive ...
- We validate ...

## Paragraph 5: Paper Organization (optional)

Briefly describe section structure.

---

## Quick quality check

- Can a reader identify your research gap in one sentence?
- Are contributions specific and measurable?
- Does the introduction avoid implementation-level details too early?
